Meet Dr. Puja Agarwal

ADA & SMDA Member, American & Southern MD Dental Assn.

Dr. Agarwal is a dedicated and dynamic general dentist with extensive experience providing expert dental treatment. Her exceptional patient skills focus on restorative treatment and preventative management — and she's an empathetic professional, capable of reassuring and alleviating patient fears.

She is enthusiastic about dental health and enjoys educating patients on care and hygiene. Outside the office, Dr. Agarwal loves her family, spending time with her husband and daughter, and taking her little one to farms and parks to stay close to nature.

  • Focused on restorative care, comfort, and preventative management.
  • Member of the American Dental Association & Southern Maryland Dental Association.
  • Named one of "America's Top Dentists" in 2019, 2020, 2021 & 2022.
